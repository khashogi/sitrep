2. MILITARY OPERATIONS

Coalition Campaign — "Next Phase": IDF Chief of Staff declared the campaign is entering its "next phase" after 2,500 strikes with 6,000+ weapons. Israel claims near-complete air superiority and destruction of 80% of Iran's air defense systems along with 300+ missile launchers. Strikes are now shifting deeper inland, hitting IRGC special forces bases (3rd Hazrat-e Mehdi Ranger Brigade in Khuzestan confirmed via satellite imagery), Bushehr International Airport (Iran Air A319 destroyed on tarmac — Rosatom suspended construction on nearby nuclear plant units and evacuated staff), and IRIB broadcast infrastructure in Tabriz.

The IDF issued forced displacement threats to residents of Qom's Shokouhiyeh industrial area — ordering civilians to leave within hours. This mirrors Gaza-style displacement operations now applied to Iranian cities. Friday saw the heaviest bombardment of Tehran yet, with strikes near Pasteur Street (home to key government institutions including the Office of the President).

Iranian Military Degradation: CENTCOM reported ballistic missile attacks down 90% and drone attacks down 83% since Day 1. This is consistent with CTP-ISW's assessment that the launcher destruction campaign has been highly effective. However, Iran retains residual capability: a hybrid drone/missile attack hit Tel Aviv Thursday night with cluster warheads. Iran also claims it struck a US oil tanker in the northern Persian Gulf (unconfirmed by CENTCOM).

Bandar Abbas Naval Port: Satellite imagery shows damage to IRGC Navy drone storage facilities, Artesh Navy dry docks, and buildings near submarine docks. Two Kilo-class submarines previously visible at the port are no longer visible on March 4 imagery — CTP-ISW cannot assess whether they were destroyed or relocated.

Lebanon Front: Israel issued a Dahiyeh-wide evacuation order for Beirut's southern suburbs — the broadest displacement order yet. 77 killed in Lebanon since conflict began, 300,000+ evacuated from the south. IDF is "pushing deeper" into Lebanon.

Azerbaijan — War Widening: Iranian drones struck Azerbaijani territory — the first attack on a non-coalition, non-Gulf state. Nakhchivan (Azerbaijani exclave bordering Iran and Turkey) was previously struck. This geographic expansion raises concerns about the conflict spreading into the South Caucasus.
